Data Deletion Instructions

If you have contacted City Cars Rentals through Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Meta ads, or another channel and want your personal information deleted, please send a deletion request using one of the contact methods below.

How To Request Deletion

Email citycarsrentals247@gmail.com or message/call +234 802 669 2722 with the subject or message "Data deletion request". Include the phone number or social profile you used when contacting us so we can locate your enquiry.

What Happens Next

We will confirm receipt, verify the request where needed, and delete or anonymize information we no longer need. Some records may be retained where required for legal, accounting, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or completed booking obligations.
