Privacy Policy

City Cars Rentals provides chauffeur and car rental services in Lagos, Nigeria. This policy explains how we handle information shared with us through our website, WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, Meta lead/message ads, phone calls, and other customer contact channels.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, phone number, pickup and destination details, booking dates, vehicle preference, quote requests, and messages you send to us. When you interact with our Meta ads, Meta may provide campaign and messaging context so we can respond to your enquiry.

How We Use Information

We use customer information to confirm availability, prepare quotes, coordinate bookings, provide customer support, improve our services, prevent fraud, and comply with legal or business record obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share necessary booking details with drivers, operations staff, payment providers, or service providers who help us deliver the rental service.

Retention

We keep booking and enquiry information only for as long as reasonably needed for service delivery, customer support, accounting, security, and legal requirements.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. We will respond as soon as reasonably possible, subject to legal or operational obligations.
